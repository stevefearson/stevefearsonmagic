Wondering who we are or why you're received mail from us?

Stevefearsonmagic.com delivers emails and magic newsletters by request to subscribers only. If you're receiving mail from us it's because you voluntarily submitted your email address at one of our affiliated sites. These sites currently include but may not be limited to Emagicsupply.com, Fearsonmagic.com and Stevefearson.com

BUT I DON'T REMEMBER SUBSCRIBING, ARE YOU SURE?

We always use double opt-in form subscription, which requires you to confirm your subscription request by clicking on a confirmation email sent to your address. This ensures that only you can subscribe to one of our lists. If you've received an email asking to confirm a subscription that you didn't request it means someone either mistakenly or purposefully submitted your email to our site. You can ignore the request and you will not be added to any list. If you receive more than one please let us know and we'll do our best to resolve the issue. We do not harvest emails from the internet or buy lists. We hate SPAM as much as you and we never send unsolicited emails.

HOW CAN I UNSUBSCRIBE?

We include an unsubscribe link in all of our mailings making it easy for you to quickly and painlessly unsubscribe from any or all of our lists. You can also send a removal request to us at any time at support@stevefearsonmagic.com
